Police Login

1. How can I report a missing person?

Go to the "Report Problem" section, select "Missing Person," and provide details like name, age, last seen location, and your contact. Upload a recent photo to help authorities act quickly.

2. How do I report a crime like theft or suspicious activity?

In the "Report Problem" section, select the crime type (Theft, Suspicious Activity, Assault, etc.), fill in all necessary details, and submit. The nearest police station will receive your report automatically.

3. Can I report anonymously?

Yes, you can report anonymously. However, providing your contact information helps authorities reach you if further details are needed for investigation.

4. What should I do if I spot a most-wanted criminal?

Do not approach the individual. Report the sighting using the "Most Wanted Criminal" option in the "Report Problem" section with details like location, time, and description.

5. How can I check the status of my report?

The status of your report is updated individually by police via phone calls. You will be contacted directly once any progress is made.
