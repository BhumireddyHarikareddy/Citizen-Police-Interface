About SecureSight

The Public Reporting & Police Verification System is designed to help citizens and authorities work together for a safer society. Our platform allows you to:

  • Track Missing Persons quickly and efficiently.
  • Stay informed about Most Wanted Criminals in your region.
  • Submit Problem Reports such as theft, suspicious activity, or other issues.

This project bridges the gap between the public and law enforcement by providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly system. Our mission is to make reporting easy, accessible, and effective for everyone.
